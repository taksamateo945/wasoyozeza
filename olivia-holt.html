Career

Holt’s acting career began in community theater. When she was ten years old, she appeared in advertisements for Hasbro, Mattel, Littlest Pet Shop, Kidz Bop 14, Girl Gourmet, and Bratz dolls.

Holt was cast in Kickin’ It, a martial arts comedy that premiered on Disney XD on June 13, 2011. He possesses gymnastics abilities. She starred as Skylar Lewis in the 2012 Disney Channel Original Movie Girl vs. Monster. She left Kickin’ It after season three to star in the Disney Channel’s half-hour comedy I Didn’t Do It.

Holt reprised their roles in the third and final season of Runaways, and Holt voiced their respective characters in the Disney XD animated series Spider-Man. Holt stars as Kate Wallis in the Freeform series Cruel Summer, which premieres on April 20, 2021.

Olivia made her acting debut in November 2014 in Michael Carney’s drama Same Kind of Different as Me. The film premiered in October 2017. Holt was cast as the lead in Eric Stoltz’s independent comedy Class Rank in November 2015. In May 2016, it was announced that she will star alongside Ross Lynch in Scott Speer’s Status Update, with production set to begin in Vancouver, Canada, the following month.

Holt collaborated on three songs and performed them in the 2012 film Girl vs. Monster. She won a 2013 Radio Disney Music Award for Best Crush Song for “Had Me @ Hello.” Additionally, she provided the theme music for the 2014 Disneynature film Bears with her song “Carry On.” Similarly, in October 2014, she reserved a record deal with Hollywood Records.

In 2016, Olivia embarked on her first tour, the Rise of a Phoenix Tour. In April 2017, she was named Elvis Duran’s Artist of the Month. On September 22, 2017, she released her song “Generous.” In 2018, she collaborated with Martin Jensen on “16 Steps” and Nicky Romero on “Distance.”

In 2020, her song “Love U Again” debuted at the top of the Billboard Hot Dance / Electronic Songs chart. Additionally, in early 2021, she released the songs “Do You Miss Me” and “Love On You.” For Cruel Summer, she covered The Smashing Pumpkins’ “Today.” On June 11, 2021, she released “In My Feelings,” a compilation EP featuring previously released tracks. Holt parted ways with Hollywood Records in November 2021.
