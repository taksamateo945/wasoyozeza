Paddy McGuinness weight loss: Top Gear star shares tips on losing 2 stone in just 4 months

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, May 24, 2024

Having often appeared on TV to present a slew of shows, Paddy knows about the pressures of looking good in front of the camera.

However, to him, feeling good is more important.

In 2019, the presenter posted before-and-after photos of his weight loss, revealing that he has dropped from 14 stone to 12.5 stone in just four months.

This was due to a change in his diet and fitness regime.
