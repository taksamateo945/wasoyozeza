Juancho Hernangezom Rise To Basketball Glory With Panathinaikos

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, May 6, 2024

In 2012, Hernangómez inked his first professional contract with CB Estudiantes.

He started competing with their second team and remained there until 2014 when he earned a promotion to the main team.

During the 2015-16 season, Hernangómez showcased his skills, averaging 9.7 points and 5.7 rebounds in 34 games.

This outstanding performance led to him being awarded the ACB Best Young Player Award.

Teams Juancho Hernangomez has played for;

  • Panathinaikos

Awards and Achievements

Juancho Hernangomez, a highly skilled and disciplined basketball player, receives guidance from his family, all of whom have professional basketball backgrounds.

He maintains a spotless record, staying clear of controversies and remaining intensely focused on the game.

His professional basketball journey has garnered him significant popularity and recognition within the community, thanks to his exceptional qualities in both his professional and personal life.

Juancho Hernangomez Net Worth

Juancho Hernangomez has represented numerous NBA franchises since he entered the league.

He commenced his NBA journey by securing a contract with the Denver Nuggets. Over time, Juancho accumulated a substantial fortune before joining the Toronto Raptors.

His current net worth stands at US$17 million, a significant increase from the estimated US$10 million in 2019.

J.Hernangomez Family

Juancho Hernangomez hails from a Spanish family with a strong basketball background.

Both his parents, Guillermo Hernangomez Heredero and Margarita Geuer, have represented national basketball teams.

His siblings, Willy Hernangomez and Andrea Hernangomez, are currently playing for the New Orleans Pelicans and Estudiantes, respectively. As for Juancho, he remains unmarried.
