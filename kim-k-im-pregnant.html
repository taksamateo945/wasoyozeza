Kim Kardashian West announces pregnancy

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, April 2, 2024

Kim Kardashian West has announced that her and her husband of one year, rap superstar Kanye West, are expecting their second child together.

The couple who already have daughter North who will turn 2 later this month, announced their news in a clip for the new series of Keeping Up With The Kardashians.

Kim is seen in the video telling her sister Khloe that she is Pregnant.
