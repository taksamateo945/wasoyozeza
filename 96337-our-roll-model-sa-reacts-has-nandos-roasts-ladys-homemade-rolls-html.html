&quot;Our Roll Model&quot;: SA Reacts Has Nando's Roasts Lady's Homemade Rolls

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, June 28, 2024

- Fast-food restaurant Nando's has once again managed to share a stunning clap-back on its popular Twitter page

- Heading online, the food giant decided to share a hilarious response to a woman's homemade "Nando's rolls"

- Many people once again headed online to share their thoughts on the hilarious post in the comment section

PAY ATTENTION: Join Briefly News' Telegram channel! Never miss important updates!

Nando's is quickly becoming a source of endless laughter and entertainment on South African Twitter as they time and time again produce the crispiest clap-backs. This time the lit social media team is guilty of roasting a woman's rolls.

It all started when the young lady with the Twitter handle, @_TlhogyM headed to the app to share some photos of what she called her very own homemade 'Nando's rolls.' Of course, Nando's did not let it slide and responded:

"Add some flour, sis! Nkare you didn't hear @rikyrickworld when he said ungazincishi?"

Very soon locals were in the comment section sharing their thoughts on Nandos' growing cheek. A lot of them praised the hardworking social media team for continuing to deliver the content they appreciate.

We previously reported that Nando's has once again managed to cause a stir online after sharing another absolutely hilarious post. It all started when a Twitter user with the handle @pontsho_mp shared a clip of a chicken that appears to be playing football.

Nando's quickly seized the opportunity to have a laugh and leave a lot of other people laughing by calling the chicken 'FerNando's Torres' - a clever play on the name of Spanish footballer, Fernando Torres. Tagging a local football team in their tweet, they wrote:

"@lidodaduvha, bona FerNando's Torres!"
