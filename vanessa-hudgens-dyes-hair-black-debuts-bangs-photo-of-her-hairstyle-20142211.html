Vanessa Hudgens Dyes Hair Black, Debuts Bangs: Photo of Her Hairstyle

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, April 13, 2024

Back to black!

Vanessa Hudgens’ hair color changes right along with the seasons. Here’s the proof: Over the summer the Spring Breakers actress, 25, sported an ombre bob with honey blonde ends.

Fast forward to Halloween in October, and the dip-dye style was long gone, replaced with a fire-engine red hue.

Dizzy yet? Now heading into winter, the star just debuted an all-black wavy bob hairstyle with bangs, reminiscent of her true dark brown hair color.

Hudgens posted a selfie of the new look with rouge lips, crediting her go-to celebrity hairstylist Riawna Capri of West Hollywood’s Nine Zero One Salon for the change.

“@riawnacpri Banged Me Good @ninezerone,” she captioned the photo. Capri, who also works with Julianne Hough and Brooke Burke-Charvet gave Hudgens a deep and rich shag with choppy bangs.
