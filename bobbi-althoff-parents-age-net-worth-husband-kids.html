Bobbi Althoff Parents: Age, Net Worth, Husband, Kids

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, July 13, 2024

Bobbi Althoff is a 26-year-old content creator, influencer, and social media personality.

She rose to stardom when she secured a groundbreaking interview with the renowned rapper Drake in July 2023 on her podcast, The Really Good Podcast.

The episode got millions of views, thanks to Drake’s massive fan base, and made her a viral sensation.

But who is Bobbi Althoff, and what is her story? In this article, we will explore her Parents, career, personal life, and more.

Bobbi Althoff Parents

Not much is known about Bobbi Althoff’s parents or siblings on social media.

She occasionally posts photos or videos of them on special occasions such as birthdays or holidays.

She told Cosmopolitan that her dad was a handyman, but she did not reveal his name or occupation.

Bobbi Althoff Age

Bobbi Althoff was born on July 31, 1997, in California, USA.

She is an American national of mixed ethnicity and has a younger sister, but the identities of her parents are unknown.

She grew up in a working-class family and graduated from high school in Southern California.

Bobbi Althoff Real Name

Bobbi Althoff’s real name is Bobbi Jo Althoff, but she uses her middle name as her stage name because she thinks it sounds more catchy and memorable.

A news publication once mistakenly referred to her as Hagrid, but this appears to be a misunderstanding of an Instagram post.

Bobbi Althoff Net Worth

Bobbi Althoff has an estimated net worth of around $4 million as of 2023.

She earns most of her income from her social media platforms, where she gets paid for sponsored posts, advertisements, and collaborations.

She also makes money from her podcast, where she gets revenue from sponsors, donations, and merchandise sales.

Bobbi Althoff Husband

Bobbi Althoff is married to Cory Althoff, a programmer who works as a senior vice president at CompTIA according to his LinkedIn profile.

He is also the author of two books, The Self-Taught Programmer (2017) and The Self-Taught Computer Scientist (2021).

They met on Bumble and got engaged in October 2019. They have two daughters, who were born in December 2019 and June 2022.

Althoff refers to her daughters as Richard and Concrete to keep their identities private.

Bobbi Althoff Kids

Althoff is a mother of two daughters, whom she calls Richard and Concrete for privacy reasons.

She does not show their faces on camera, but she occasionally posts photos or videos of them on special occasions such as birthdays or holidays.

She also shares hilarious and sarcastic tips about parenting and motherhood on her TikTok account.

Bobbi Althoff Drake

One of the most notable events in Bobbi Althoff’s career was her interview with Drake in July 2023 on her podcast, The Really Good Podcast.

The episode showed the two of them lying under the covers of a bed while having a very awkward and uncomfortable conversation.

The video went viral on TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, and other platforms, gaining millions of views and making her a household name.

The interview came about after Drake saw one of her videos with Funny Marco, another comedian known for his dry humor.

He liked the video and followed her on Instagram.

She then sent him a DM asking if he wanted to be on her podcast.

Surprisingly, he said yes and sent over his touring schedule. She quickly booked a flight to Memphis and met him at his hotel.

The interview was full of hilarious moments such as Drake asking her if she was nervous, her telling him that he looked like an eggplant emoji, and him asking her to rate his music from one to ten.

She also asked him personal questions such as his favorite color, his zodiac sign, and his relationship status.

However, the interview also sparked some rumors and controversies.

Althoff denied all these allegations and said that nothing happened between them other than the interview.

However, shortly after the interview was released, she deleted all the content related to Drake from her social media accounts and unfollowed him on Instagram.

He also unfollowed her and did not promote the interview on his platforms.

Conclusion

Bobbi Althoff is a rising star in the social media and podcasting world.

She has gained a huge following and a lot of attention for her unique and humorous interviews with celebrities.

She has also shown her versatility and creativity as a content creator, influencer, and comedian.

Bobbi Althoff has a bright future ahead of her and we can’t wait to see what she does next.
