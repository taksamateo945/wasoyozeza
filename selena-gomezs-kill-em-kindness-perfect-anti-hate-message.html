Selena Gomez's 'Kill Em With Kindness' is the Perfect Anti-Hate Message

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, January 30, 2024

Right now the world seems to be filled with hate, but it is important to remember that the world is also full of wonderful people who believe regardless of gender, sexuality, race, nationality or religion, everybody deserves love and respect.

One of those beautiful people spreading love to the world is Selena Gomez, who’s new single ‘Kill Em With Kindness’ is helping spread the message of love and positivity all over the world.

The lyrics of the song are so meaningful and show just how caring Selena is.

It’s a shame that there are still people in this world who don’t share the same outlook, and are instead intent on spreading hate, but like the lyrics say “we don’t have to fall from grace”.

Just because someone is cruel or mean it doesn’t mean you have to be as well.

Always remember we are a family, be kind to one another and spread love not hate.

We are all the same, we are all humans. We should all stand united and live side by side in peace.
