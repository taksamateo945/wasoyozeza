Top Facts about James Herbert

  • James Herbert was a best-selling English horror writer.
  • He wrote 23 novels, including “The Rats” and “The Fog.”
  • His books have sold over 54 million copies worldwide.
  • Herbert won the British Fantasy Award three times.
  • He was appointed Officer of the Order of the British Empire (OBE) in 2010.
  • Herbert’s first novel, “The Rats,” was rejected by several publishers before being accepted.
  • He also wrote three non-fiction books about ghosts and hauntings.
  • Herbert died in 2013 at the age of 69 from a heart attack.
  • His final novel, “Ash,” was published posthumously in 2013.
  • Many consider him to be one of England’s greatest horror writers ever.
