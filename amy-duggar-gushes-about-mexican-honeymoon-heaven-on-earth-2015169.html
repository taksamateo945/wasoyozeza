Amy Duggar Gushes About Mexican Honeymoon: &quot;Heaven on Earth!&quot;

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, January 18, 2024

Hola! Amy Duggar’s honeymoon with new husband Dillion King has been “heaven on Earth,” the newlywed gushed exclusively to Us Weekly in the new issue.

“Mexico is amazing!” Jim Bob and Michelle Duggar’s niece told Us of Cancun, where she’s been enjoying some R&R for the past week. “If we aren’t taking a dip in the ocean, then we are enjoying our private terrace and splash pool!” 

The lovebirds, who have been noshing on “delicious” food and cocktails since arriving at Excellence Playa Mujeres hotel, said “I do” in front of 400 friends and family on Sept. 6 in Betonville, Ark.

The 19 Kids and Counting cousin donned a cleavage-baring strapless gown by Essence of Australia to wed Dillion, whom she’s been dating since November 2014. "I loved how modern it was, and I love the train," she told Us of her gown. "I love everything about it. I would wear it to the grocery store and the gas station. I might bowl in it. Go-karts…I'll find a way to wear it all the time."
