Khloe Kardashian Seemingly Blasts Scott Disick, Supports Kourtney

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, February 4, 2024

Thicker than water! Khloe Kardashian appears to have subtly — and pretty much perfectly — blasted Scott Disick on Instagram following her sister Kourtney Kardashian’s split from the self-proclaimed Lord.

The youngest Kardashian, who joined her newly single sibling and older sis Kim Kardashian for dinner at Casa Escobar in Los Angeles on Tuesday, July 7, captioned a group selfie with her sisters sticking out their tongues: “IDFWU!!!!!”

The caption, of course, refers to Big Sean’s hit track — and breakup anthem — “I Don’t F—k With You.”

As Keeping Up With the Kardashians fans are well aware, Khloe, 31, was especially close with Disick, 32, who, after getting uncomfortably close with his ex-girlfriend Chloe Bartoli while partying in the South of France this weekend, is no longer dating the reality star, 36.

Kourtney, for her part, appeared confident and looked happy to be out with her sisters, who also joined the mom of three for a morning workout earlier in the day. “Sister workout sesh this morning in the rain,” Kourtney, who looked noticeably slimmed-down in the snap, captioned a photo of the clan sweatin’ it out together.

“She really has had it this time,” an insider previously told Us of Kourtney’s decision to finally end things with Disick, who was observed as being a “drunken mess” while cozying up to his ex in Monaco last week. 

“Honestly, when I saw those pictures, I just went, ‘Uh-oh.’” Kourtney’s stepbrother Brody Jenner admitted on the Today show Tuesday. “I even showed my girlfriend. I just went, ‘This isn’t good.'”
