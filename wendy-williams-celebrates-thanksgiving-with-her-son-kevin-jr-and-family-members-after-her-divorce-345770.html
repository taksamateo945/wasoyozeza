Wendy Williams Celebrates Thanksgiving With Her Son Kevin Jr. And Family Members After Her Divorce!

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, August 19, 2024

Wendy Williams just came out of a two-decades-long marriage which means that this Thanksgiving was spent as a single woman for the first time in a very long time. That being said, it looks like the talk show host had a great time during the holiday since she spent it with her son, Kevin Hunter Jr. and other members of her family!

Wendy took to her social media platform of choice to share some pics documenting her family time in Miami and in the caption, she wrote: ‘Nothing feels better than food for the soul!’

This marked her first Thanksgiving since filing for divorce and judging by the post, it doesn’t look like she was thinking of the failed relationship and the cheating at all!

The celeb had her closest family members, including her 19 year old son Kevin Jr. there with her so the house was filled with loved ones, great food and excitement.

And since they were in Miami, Wendy also made sure that she got some sun before eating some more turkey!

‘Some @ the table some @ the poolside…but love is all around,’ she also wrote.

You probably know that Wendy and Kevin were married for no less than 23 years before she decided to end things for good in April after finding out that he had been having an affair.

Not only that but Kevin also fathered a baby girl with mistress Sharina Hudson.

Wendy has been refraining from slamming her husband too much publicly but she did stress that the one thing she could not forgive him for was having a child with someone else.

And since that is exactly what he did, Wendy had no choice but file for divorce.
