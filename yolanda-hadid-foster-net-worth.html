Yolanda Hadid Foster Net Worth: A Journey from Dutch Farm Girl to Global Success

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, June 18, 2024

Yolanda Hadid Foster, a former model, television personality, and entrepreneur from the Netherlands, enjoyed a diverse and successful career.

She earned millions through her appearances on the reality TV show “The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills” and her endeavors as an interior designer and skincare entrepreneur.

Early Life

Yolanda van den Herik, later known as Yolanda Hadid Foster, entered the world on January 11, 1964.

Her upbringing unfolded in a quaint Dutch town where her days were filled with horseback riding, cow milking, and various farm tasks.

Alongside her was her brother, Leo. Tragically, when Yolanda was a mere seven years old, her father died in a car accident.

This ordeal posed a substantial challenge for the entire family, but Yolanda’s mother, determined to provide for her children, embraced newfound independence.

Yolanda herself grew resolute about contributing to her family’s well-being.

At the tender age of 13, she embarked on her first job, washing dishes at a Chinese restaurant.

Additionally, she took on employment at a local grocery store.

Remarkably, this young girl managed to balance her responsibilities at work, school, and the demands of farm life.

What is Yolanda Hadid Foster Net Worth and Salary?

Yolanda Hadid, a model and reality television personality, boasts a substantial net worth of $45 million.

She achieved fame and amassed her fortune independently during the 1980s as a model.

Throughout her journey, she garnered significant attention for her high-profile relationships with renowned figures like Julio Iglesias, Mohamed Hadid, and David Foster.

Today, she primarily garners recognition for her role on the reality TV series “The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills” and for being the mother of supermodels Bella Hadid and Gigi Hadid.
