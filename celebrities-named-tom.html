22 Famous People and Celebrities Named Tom

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, February 25, 2024

When it comes to celebrities and other famous people, many of us take an interest in seeing if there’s anyone out there with the same name. If you’re named Tom, for example, chances are you’ve spent some time looking up all the famous Toms out there!

There are plenty of reasons why people like to research celebrity doppelgängers. For one thing, it can be a great source of entertainment — after all, who doesn’t love playing a game where they search for famous figures that share their name? It also helps connect us to celebrities and public figures we may not have otherwise known about.

22 of the Coolest Famous People with the Name Tom

Finding someone with the same name as you can give a sense of connection and identity. We all want to feel like someone else out there understands us, and having a well-known celebrity with the same name as your own helps reinforce that bond. View the full list of Toms below.

Tom Holland

Holland is an English actor and dancer who has made a name for himself after appearing in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). He made his debut in 2016’s Captain America: Civil War. Since then, he has starred as Spider-Man in two MCU movies and the Sony Pictures spinoff, Spider-Man: Homecoming. In recent years, Holland has also appeared in films such as The Lost City of Z (2017) and Chaos Walking (2021).

Tom Felton

Felton is an English actor best known for playing Draco Malfoy in the Harry Potter franchise. Since appearing in the series, he has gone on to star in other projects such as Rise of the Planet of the Apes (2011), Murder in the First (2014–2016), Feed (2017), and Origin (2018). More recently, he appeared alongside Tom Holland in the 2020 MCU movie, Spider-Man: Far From Home.

Tom Brady

Brady is an American football quarterback who has been playing in the National Football League (NFL) since 2000. He currently plays for the Tampa Bay Buccaneers and holds a number of records, including most Super Bowl titles (7). In addition, he has won multiple awards such as NFL MVP and NFL Offensive Player of the Year.

Tom Cruise

Cruise is an American actor, producer, and philanthropist who rose to fame after appearing in several iconic 1980s movies like Top Gun (1986). Since then, he has starred in numerous other films like Jerry Maguire (1996), Mission Impossible franchise (1996–present), and Edge of Tomorrow (2014). Cruise has also been awarded three Golden Globe Awards, including Best Actor for Born on the Fourth of July (1989)

Tom Hanks

Hanks is an American actor, director, producer, and writer who has received numerous awards throughout his career. He is best known for appearing in films such as Forrest Gump (1994), The Green Mile (1999), Cast Away (2000), Saving Private Ryan (1998), and Apollo 13 (1995). In addition, he has won two Academy Awards for Best Actor.

Tom Hiddleston

Hiddleston is an English actor who gained recognition after starring in the Marvel Cinematic Universe movies Thor (2011) and Avengers: Infinity War (2018). He has also appeared in films such as Midnight in Paris (2011), Kong: Skull Island (2017), and High Rise (2015). In addition, he has won a number of awards including an Emmy for his role as Loki.

Tom Hardy

Hardy is an English actor and producer who made a name for himself after starring in the blockbuster movie The Dark Knight Rises (2012). Since then, he has gone on to appear in other films such as Mad Max: Fury Road (2015) and Venom (2018). In 2020, he starred alongside Tom Holland in the Sony Pictures spinoff, Spider-Man: Homecoming.

Tom Kenny

Kenny is an American voice actor and comedian who is best known for providing the voice of the title character in the Nickelodeon cartoon, SpongeBob SquarePants. In addition to SpongeBob, he has provided voices for characters in cartoons such as Adventure Time (2010–2015), The Powerpuff Girls (1998–2005), and Rocko’s Modern Life (1993–1996).

Tom Jones

Jones is a Welsh singer who achieved international fame during the 1960s with hits like “It’s Not Unusual” and “What’s New Pussycat?”. He has released over 30 albums throughout his career and continues to perform live concerts around the world. He has also been awarded several honors including a Grammy Lifetime Achievement Award.

Tom Selleck

Selleck is an American actor and film producer who gained recognition for starring in the television series Magnum P.I. (1980–1988). After appearing in this show, he has gone on to star in movies such as Three Men and a Baby (1987), Quigley Down Under (1990), and Jesse Stone film series (2005–2015).

Tom Petty

Petty was an American singer-songwriter and musician best known as the lead vocalist of Tom Petty and the Heartbreakers. He released numerous hit songs throughout his career including “Free Fallin'”, “American Girl”, and “I Won’t Back Down”. He posthumously received a Grammy Lifetime Achievement Award in 2018.

Tom Sizemore

Sizemore is an American actor and producer who has appeared in films like Saving Private Ryan (1998), Black Hawk Down (2001), Pearl Harbor (2001), and Red Planet (2000). He has also starred in the television series, Crash (2008–2009) and Hawaii Five-0 (2010–2013).

Tom Sandoval

Sandoval is an American reality star who first gained recognition after appearing on Bravo’s hit television series, Vanderpump Rules. Since then, he has gone on to appear in movies such as Mischief Night (2014) and The Gourmet Detective: Eat, Drink & Be Buried (2017). He also owns several restaurants in Los Angeles.

Tom Welling

Welling is an American actor, director and producer best known for portraying Clark Kent in the hit television series Smallville (2001-2011). He has also appeared in movies such as Cheaper By The Dozen (2003), The Fog (2005) and Draft Day (2014). In addition to acting, he runs his own production company.

Tom Cavanagh

Cavanagh is a Canadian actor who has gained recognition after appearing on several television shows including Scrubs (2001–2010), Ed (2000–2004), and Trust Me (2009–2010). He currently stars as Harrison Wells in The Flash TV series. In addition to acting, he is a talented musician and recorded an album in 2007.

Tom Hopper

Hopper is an English actor best known for his roles as Billy Bones in the Starz series Black Sails (2014-2017) and Dickon Tarly in the hit HBO series Game of Thrones (2016-2019). He has also appeared in other television shows such as Merlin (2008–2012) and The Umbrella Academy (2019–present).

Tom Bergeron

Bergeron is an American game show host who gained recognition after hosting America’s Funniest Home Videos from 2001 to 2015. Since then, he has gone on to host a number of different programs including Dancing with the Stars (since 2005) and Hollywood Squares (1998 – 2004).

Tom Schwartz

Schwartz is an American reality star who first gained recognition after appearing on Bravo’s hit television series Vanderpump Rules. He currently owns a restaurant, TomTom, with his co-stars Tom Sandoval and Lisa Vanderpump.

Tom Ford

Ford is an American fashion designer best known for his work at Gucci and Yves Saint Laurent. He has also directed several films such as A Single Man (2009) and Nocturnal Animals (2016). In addition to designing clothes, he is also the creative director of Estée Lauder’s beauty brand, Tom Ford Beauty.

Tom Green

Green is a Canadian comedian and actor best known for hosting The Tom Green Show from 1994 to 2000. He has also appeared in movies such as Freddy Got Fingered (2001), Road Trip (2000) and Shred (2008).

Tom Clancy

Clancy was an American novelist best known for his military thrillers like The Hunt for Red October (1984), Patriot Games (1987) and Clear and Present Danger (1989). His works were so popular that they have been adapted into several films, video games, television series’ and more.

Tom Segura

Segura is an American stand-up comedian who gained recognition after appearing on the Netflix show, Disgraceful which he co-created alongside Bert Kreischer. He currently hosts a podcast called Your Mom’s House with his wife, Christina Pazsitzky. He has also released five comedy specials on Netflix.
