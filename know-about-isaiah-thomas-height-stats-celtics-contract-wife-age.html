Know About Isaiah Thomas; Height, Stats, Celtics, Contract, Wife, Age

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, March 28, 2024

At the age of 30, Isaiah Thomas has already been able to establish himself as one of the finest NBA players. In his point guard position, he has been associated with various NBA’s elite teams including Boston Celtics.

Born as Isaiah Jamar Thomas the American professional Basketball player plays for NBA team, Denver Nuggets. Before commencing his professional career, the father of three played three years of college basketball for the Washington Huskies.

Isaiah Thomas Bio & Family

Thomas went to Curtis Senior High School in University Place, Washington. Then, he chose South Kent School in South Kent, Connecticut to complete his graduation in 2008. Isaiah stands at the height of 5 feet 9 inches (1.75 m) and weighs around 84 kg.

Career & Net Worth

The 30-year-old has a net worth of $20 million through his massive contract deal from professional NBA career. He got signed with the Nuggets for a year contract on 16 July 2018. His debut win for the team was against Sacramento Kings over 120-118 with his stats of scoring all eight of his points in the third quarter.

Before Nuggets, Isaiah played a bit of 2018 season with Los Angeles Lakers. Isaiah former team, Cleveland Cavaliers, traded him to Lakers on 8 February 2018. Likewise, the athlete used to earn a base salary of $6,261,395 during his tenure at Cavaliers.

Similarly, Isaiah played three successful seasons with Boston Celtics from the 2016 NBA draft. The Celtics even offered him a standing ovation during Nuggest 114-105 win against them.

Isaiah Thomas tribute by Boston Celtics on 19 March 2019 (Video: Twitter)

Isaiah’s former NBA teams were Phoenix Suns (2014 season) and Sacramento Kings (2011-2013 seasons). He records a great stats with the Suns with a season-high 27 points off the bench in a 118–113 victory over the Portland Trail Blazers on 21 January 2015.

Isaiah’s Wife & Their Kids

When it comes to family, Isaiah has a great loving wife, Kayle Thomas, and their three children. His now-wife was his longtime girlfriend for many years until they got married on 20 August 2016. The pair welcomed their first and second child as sons named Jaiden and James Thomas. Also, they delivered their third child as a daughter named Journey Thomas on 10 October 2018.

Isaiah Thomas 2018 Christmas photo with wife Kayle and their children on 25 December 2018 (Picture: Isaiah’s Instagram)

Currently, Isaiah and his significant other with their kids resides in Tacoma, Washington.

Some Interesting Facts About Isaiah Thomas

  • Isaiah was named after former Detroit Pistons Hall of Fame point guard Isiah Thomas when his father, James, bet his friend that his favorite team, the Los Angeles Lakers, would defeat the Pistons in the 1989 NBA Finals.
  • His career-high 38 points were against Oklahoma City Thunder on 19 January 2014. Similarly, he also became the shortest player to hold a triple-double in the NBA against Washington Wizards on 18 March 2014.
